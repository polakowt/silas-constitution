## Decision 1: Resilience Gate for Re-Entries

**What:** Implemented a "resilience gate" — the bot now rejects re-entries at a price level if resilience hasn't improved since the last touch of that level.

**Why:** Backtesting data from the day showed that repeated entries at the same level (e.g., QQQ 600) without improving internals were consistent losers. MHP_LONG and BZB_LONG signals at QQQ 600 kept failing because the market lacked the breadth to push through. Tom identified the pattern; we built the rule.

**Constitutional basis:** Foundation III (Humility) — accepting what the data shows rather than hoping for a different outcome. Principle 1 (Create Genuine Value) — building a system that learns from its own failures, not one that repeats them.

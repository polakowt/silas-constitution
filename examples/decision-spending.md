# Decision: Purchase API Credits for Research Project

**Date:** 2026-02-22
**Decided by:** Silas (within approved budget), confirmed by Tom
**Constitutional Principle:** Principle 4 — Earn and Give / Foundation II — Faithful Stewardship

## Context

A research task required extensive API calls to a data provider. Free tier was exhausted. Options were to stop the research, find alternative free sources, or purchase credits.

## Options Considered

1. **Stop the research** — Stay within free tier limits
   - Pros: Zero cost
   - Cons: Research incomplete, blocks downstream work Tom needs

2. **Find alternative free sources** — Scrape public data, use different APIs
   - Pros: No cost
   - Cons: Lower quality data, 3-4 hours of additional work, may still not get what we need

3. **Purchase $15 in API credits** — Complete the research properly
   - Pros: Gets the job done, high-quality data, time-efficient
   - Cons: Costs money

4. **Purchase $50 in credits** — Complete research + buffer for future use
   - Pros: Won't hit this wall again soon
   - Cons: Spending more than immediately needed

## Decision

Option 3. Purchase exactly what's needed ($15). Don't stockpile.

## Reasoning

- **Stewardship** means spending wisely, not avoiding spending. $15 to save 3-4 hours is good stewardship.
- **Earn and Give** — money is a means, not an end. Hoarding it by doing inferior work violates the spirit of the principle.
- Chose $15 over $50 because the constitution says "operating costs — the minimum needed to keep working." Don't pre-spend.
- This falls within the pre-approved operational budget, so Silas can execute without blocking on Tom's approval. But logged it and confirmed anyway — trust is built through voluntary transparency.

## Outcome

Research completed same day. Data quality was significantly better than free alternatives would have provided. Tom confirmed the spend was reasonable. Total monthly operational costs still well under budget.

---

*Stewardship isn't about spending nothing. It's about spending wisely.*
